About Us

THE DANQUAH & FRIENDS FOUNDATION

The DANQUAH & Friends Foundation is a non-governmental organization founded by Emmanuel Owusu Danquah in 2018. The foundation aims to support orphans, widows, and underprivileged individuals within communities. Its first official donation was made at the Village of Hope Orphanage Home in Gomoa Fetteh. The foundation provides assistance in various forms, including paying medical bills for patients unable to afford treatment. To date, it has positively impacted the lives of over 1,500 people through its charitable initiatives. In recent times, the DANQUAH & Friends Foundation has donated over 1,200 exercise books to needy but brilliant students.

The foundation is also developing initiatives to empower underprivileged orphans and widows by providing them with opportunities to learn various trades.
